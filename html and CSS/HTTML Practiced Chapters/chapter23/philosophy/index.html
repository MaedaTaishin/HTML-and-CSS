Commitment

We are extremely particular about just one cup of coffee.

Carefully selected coffee beans.

Only the best and best coffee beans are roasted and used to provide our customers with truly delicious coffee. Also, roasted coffee beans lose their taste over time, so at Dummy Cafe, coffee we serve that day uses only the coffee beans that were roasted that day.

Hand drip at 93 ° C.

optimum temperature for hot water to extract good coffee is 93 ° C. At Dummy Cafe, we are particular about this 93 ℃ and extract coffee. Of course, we do not use a machine and carefully extract coffee one cup at a time by hand drip and provide it to our customers.
